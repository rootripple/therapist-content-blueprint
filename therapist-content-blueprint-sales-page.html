If you're unsure which to choose, the bundle is the right call.

Do I need a big following or an existing audience to use this?

No. The Blueprint is designed for therapists starting from zero or near-zero online presence. It focuses on owned content — your website, blog, and email list — and organic visibility through platforms like Psychology Today and Google, not follower counts.

I already bought the Template Pack separately. Do I need to pay full price for the bundle?

No. Email after purchasing the bundle and the price of the Template Pack will be credited toward your bundle purchase. The two products work together — buying one is the natural starting point for the other.

Is this ethically appropriate for licensed clinicians?

Yes. The Blueprint was written with clinical ethics in mind. It reframes content creation as psychoeducation and community education — a way to serve your future clients before they've booked a session, not a way to solicit them. All templates are written to reflect professional boundaries and avoid language that could feel exploitative or misleading.

How much time will this actually take each week?

The Blueprint is built around a 1–2 hours per week ceiling. The goal is a sustainable system — not a second job. The template pack exists precisely so that "writing time" becomes "personalization time," which is significantly faster.

What format does everything come in?

The Blueprint is a PDF. The Template Pack is delivered as a PDF and an editable Google Doc, so you can duplicate and use each template directly without reformatting. Everything is accessible immediately after purchase via Gumroad.
